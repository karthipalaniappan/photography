I'm Karthi Palaniappan

a professional advertising and commercial photographer with a passion for creating bold, refined, and impactful visuals. With a background in engineering and over 6 years of experience behind the lens, I blend technical precision with creative storytelling to craft images that not only look beautiful but also drive results.

My work specializes in product, food and fashion photography, helping brands build identity, connect with their audiences, and stand out in a crowded marketplace. Every project I take on is approached with a deep understanding of brand voice, visual strategy, and attention to detail — because in advertising, every pixel matters.


I've had the privilege of working with established brands like Sowbaghya, Gopuram Manjal & Kumkum, and Indus Life Sciences, delivering high-quality content that enhances brand presence and consumer engagement across platforms.

Whether it’s a high-concept commercial campaign, a sleek product shoot, or an editorial fashion series, I approach every frame as an opportunity to elevate the ordinary into something extraordinary.
