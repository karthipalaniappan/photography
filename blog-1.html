Casual Saree Portrait Shoot with Hima Bindhu

PUBLISHED ON MAY 30, 2025

Exploring Indoor and Outdoor Portraits in the Chennai Summer

As a photographer based in Chennai, I’m always looking for ways to adapt my shoots to the local climate. Summer here can be intensely hot, especially for outdoor sessions. So for this casual saree portrait shoot with model Hima Bindhu, I planned a blend of indoor and outdoor setups — all in the same beautiful black cotton saree featuring subtle floral designs.

Indoor Studio Portraits – Playing with Light and Shadows

Given the harsh summer sun, I opted to begin the shoot indoors, focusing on headshots and mid-length portraits. I used a simple empty studio wall as the background, but added creative flair by using hard light to cast dramatic shadows. To break the monotony of the plain wall, I used bogos to introduce a subtle background pattern that added visual interest without distracting from the subject.

Hima’s look was minimal and elegant, with nude makeup that highlighted her natural beauty. I experimented with a variety of poses to capture a range of expressions and moods. The headshots especially brought out the intricate details of her makeup and the soft texture of the cotton saree.

Outdoor Natural Light Portraits – A Quick Sunset Session

Despite the heat, I didn’t want to miss the opportunity to capture Hima in natural light, so we did a short outdoor session during the softer evening hours. The natural light beautifully complemented the earthy tones of her saree and added a gentle glow to the overall composition.

This part of the shoot was spontaneous and relaxed, aiming to capture the mood and flow of a breezy summer evening. Hima’s graceful movements and serene expressions came through effortlessly in these portraits.

This shoot was a perfect example of how versatile a single outfit can be when paired with thoughtful lighting and styling. From bold shadows indoors to the soft embrace of natural light outdoors, the results brought out both the strength and elegance of the traditional saree in a modern, casual portrait setting.
